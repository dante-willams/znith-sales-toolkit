Contrast fix — the biggest change from v1
V1's faint text (rgba white at 20% on #0e0d0b) had a contrast ratio of approximately 1.6:1 — failing WCAG AA by nearly 3x. Every label, placeholder, version number, and module stage descriptor in v1 was technically unreadable. The new faint value (#718096 on white) is 3.8:1, meeting AA for large text and coming close to AA for body text.
